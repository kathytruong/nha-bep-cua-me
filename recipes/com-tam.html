Cơm Tấm

Broken Rice

From Mẹ Thịnh

Category
Cơm & Xôi

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • Regular long-grain rice
  • Water — to the first crease of your pointer finger when resting on the rice

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. To make broken rice from regular rice: soak the rice for 15 minutes.
  2. Rinse twice.
  3. Massage the rice gently to break the grains.
  4. Cook using water to the level of half the first crease of your pointer finger — the usual Vietnamese finger measure.
