Steamed Pork & Egg Loaf

From Mẹ Thịnh

Serves 4

Category
Món Mặn

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • ¼ lb ground pork
  • ¼ onion, chopped
  • 3 whole eggs plus 1 egg white — reserve the yolk to spread on top of the loaf
  • Nam meo (wood ear mushroom) — one pinch, soaked until expanded
  • Bún tàu (mung bean thread noodles) — one pinch, soaked 10 minutes
  • Salt, pepper, and chicken base, to taste

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Line a pan with plastic wrap for easy removal.
  2. Mix all ingredients together and pour into the pan.
  3. Spread the reserved egg yolk on top.
  4. Cover the pan tightly with foil.
  5. Steam for about 30 minutes, until the mixture is solid but not dry.
