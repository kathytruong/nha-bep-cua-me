Bì

Shredded Pork Skin for Cơm Tấm

From Mẹ Thịnh

Category
Cơm & Xôi

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork shoulder
  • Salt and garlic powder (about 1 tbsp)
  • Que Huong Bi Tuoi (7 oz) — buy frozen; do not buy dry, which must be soaked first
  • Kim Tu Thap Thinh Viet Nam (toasted rice powder)

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Season pork shoulder with salt and garlic powder.
  2. Pan-fry the pork shoulder, then slice into thin strips.
  3. Use the full bag of bì. Wash and dry thoroughly.
  4. Shred the cooked pork, bì, and rice powder together until evenly mixed.
  5. Serve with cơm tấm and thịt nướng.
