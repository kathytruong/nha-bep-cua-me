Cá Chim Trắng Nướng

Grilled White Pomfret

From Mẹ — Oanh

Category
Món Mặn

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

Nước chấm · Dipping Sauce

  • Fish sauce (nước mắm)
  • Lemongrass (sả), finely minced
  • Fresh chili peppers (ớt), finely chopped
  • Garlic (tỏi), minced

Để ăn kèm · To Serve

  • Rice vermicelli (bún)
  • Rice paper (bánh tráng)
  • Fresh herbs and lettuce

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Score the pomfret with 3 diagonal cuts on each side — this lets the marinade soak deep into the flesh and helps it cook evenly.
  2. Marinate the fish with soy sauce, cooking wine, ginger, and scallion pieces. Let it sit for about 15 minutes.
  3. Place the fish in a large oven-safe dish and put in the oven.
  4. After 5 minutes, drizzle 1 more spoon of soy sauce over the fish. Bake for another 5 minutes or until the fish is cooked through.
  5. Make the dipping sauce: mix fish sauce with lemongrass (sả), chili (ớt), and garlic (tỏi).
  6. Serve with rice vermicelli (bún), rice paper (bánh tráng), and the dipping sauce.
