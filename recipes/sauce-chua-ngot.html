Sauce Chua Ngọt

Sweet & Sour Sauce

From Mẹ — Oanh

Prep Time
5 min
Cook Time
10 min
Servings
~50 pieces
Category
Nước Chấm

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons tương xì muội (plum sauce)
  • 2 tablespoons dark corn syrup
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce (nước tương)
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar (dấm)
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch (bột bắp)
  • 5 tablespoons ketchup
  • 4 tablespoons sugar (đường)
  • ⅓ teaspoon MSG (bột ngọt)
  • ½ teaspoon salt (muối)
  • Water, to taste (tùy gia giảm)
  • 2–3 cloves garlic (tỏi), minced
  • Ground chili (ớt xay), to taste

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the cornstarch with a splash of water until smooth — this prevents lumps when you add it to the sauce later.
  2. Combine the plum sauce, dark corn syrup, soy sauce, vinegar, ketchup, sugar, MSG, and salt in a saucepan. Add water to adjust the consistency (tùy gia giảm — adjust to taste).
  3. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Add the minced garlic and ground chili.
  4. Stir in the cornstarch slurry. Keep stirring as the sauce thickens — it should become glossy and coat the back of a spoon nicely. If it's too thick, add a little more water.
  5. Taste and adjust the balance of sweet, sour, and salty.
  6. Serve warm alongside fried shrimp cakes (bánh tôm chiên) or other fried dishes. This batch makes enough for about 50 bánh tôm chiên.
