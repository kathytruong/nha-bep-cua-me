Đậu Câu Nước Dừa

Coconut Water Jelly

From Mẹ — Oanh

Prep Time
10 min
Cook Time
15 min + chill
Servings
6–8
Category
Chè

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 1 fresh coconut — use the water inside (nước dừa tươi), about 1–1.5 cups
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut cream (nước cốt dừa)
  • ¾ cup sugar (đường), or to taste
  • Agar-agar powder (bột rau câu)
  • Pinch of salt

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Layer 1: Crack open the fresh coconut and pour the water into a pot. Add the cup of water and the sugar. Add the agar-agar powder and a pinch of salt.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly until the agar-agar dissolves completely. Stir well until fully dissolved.
  3. Pour into a mold and let the first layer begin to set.
  4. Layer 2: Heat the coconut cream. Once the first layer has partially set, pour the coconut cream layer on top.
  5. Let it cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until fully set, about 2–3 hours.
  6. Once set, cut into pieces or unmold onto a plate. Serve chilled.
