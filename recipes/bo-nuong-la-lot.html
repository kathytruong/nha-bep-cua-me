Bò Nướng Lá Lốt

Beef Wrapped in Betel Leaves

From Mẹ — Oanh

Prep Time
30 min
Cook Time
15 min
Servings
4–6
Category
Nướng

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (27% fat)
  • 5 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • Lemongrass (sả), use a lot, finely minced
  • 1–2 fresh chili peppers, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon MSG (bột ngọt)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Five-spice powder (ngũ vị hương)
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 large bunch betel leaves (lá lốt), washed and patted dry

Để ăn kèm · To Serve

  • Rice vermicelli (bún)
  • Rice paper (bánh tráng)
  • Fresh herbs — lettuce, perilla (tía tô), mint
  • Pickled carrots and daikon (đồ chua)
  • Nước chấm dipping sauce

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef with garlic, lemongrass, chili, onion, soy sauce, salt, MSG, sugar, and black pepper. Mix thoroughly with your hands until everything is well incorporated. Let it marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Take a betel leaf, shiny side down. Place about 1 tablespoon of the beef mixture near the stem end. Roll it up tightly, tucking in the sides as you go — like a tiny burrito. The leaf should wrap around at least once. Secure with a toothpick if needed.
  3. Repeat until all the beef is used.
  4. Bake at 400°F, turning occasionally, until the beef is cooked through and the leaves are slightly charred and fragrant.
  5. Serve with rice vermicelli, fresh herbs, pickled vegetables, and nước chấm. Wrap everything in rice paper.
