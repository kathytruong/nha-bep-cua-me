Muối Dưa Giá

Pickled Bean Sprouts

From Mẹ — Oanh

Category
Đồ Chua

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 1 lb bean sprouts (giá), washed and drained
  • 1 bunch chives (hẹ), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 carrot (cà rốt), peeled and julienned
  • 1 tablespoon salt (muối)
  • A small pinch of alum (phèn chua) — this keeps the bean sprouts crunchy
  • Water, enough to submerge

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Wash the bean sprouts and drain well. Peel and julienne the carrot into thin matchsticks. Cut the chives into pieces.
  2. Toss the bean sprouts, chives, and carrot together in a large bowl with the salt and the pinch of alum. Mix everything together.
  3. Pack the mixture tightly into a clean glass jar. Press down firmly.
  4. Add enough warm water to just cover the vegetables. Place a weight on top to keep everything submerged.
  5. Cover and leave at room temperature for about 3 days until sour.
  6. Transfer to the refrigerator once it's sour enough. Serve as a side dish.
