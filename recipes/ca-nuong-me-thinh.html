Baked & Broiled Catfish

From Mẹ Thịnh

Category
Món Mặn

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • Catfish
  • Garlic powder
  • Black pepper
  • Chicken base
  • Oil

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Rub the stomach of the catfish with garlic powder, pepper, and chicken base. Spread with oil.
  2. Bake at 375°F for 40 minutes.
  3. Baste again with oil and broil for 12 minutes, until the skin is crispy.
