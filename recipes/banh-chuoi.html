Bánh Chuối Nướng

Banana Cake

From Mẹ — Oanh

Prep Time
20 min
Cook Time
45 min
Servings
10–12
Category
Bánh

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 medium zucchini, grated
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped dates or raisins
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9x5-inch loaf pans or one large bundt pan.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar together until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes. Gradually add the oil, mixing until well combined.
  3. Mash the banana until smooth and grate the zucchini. Add both to the egg mixture along with the vanilla. Mix well — the zucchini adds incredible moisture that Mẹ loved about this recipe.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring just until combined. Do not overmix.
  6. Fold in the chopped dates or raisins. Mẹ sometimes used both.
  7. Divide the batter between the prepared pans, filling each about two-thirds full.
  8. Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. The top should be golden brown and springy to the touch.
  9. Let cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Slice and serve warm or at room temperature. This cake keeps well wrapped — it gets even more moist the next day.
