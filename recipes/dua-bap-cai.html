Dưa Bắp Cải

Pickled Cabbage

From Mẹ — Oanh

Category
Đồ Chua

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 800g cabbage (bắp cải), about 1 small head
  • 200g carrots (cà rốt), peeled
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 80g sugar (đường)
  • 60g salt (muối)
  • 1 liter filtered water (nước lọc)
  • 1 liter rice water (nước vo gạo) — the starchy water from rinsing rice

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Separate the cabbage leaves and tear or cut them into large pieces. Julienne or thinly slice the carrots. Slice the onion into thin rings. Place the cabbage in a large basin of water and soak briefly, then drain well.
  2. Combine the filtered water and rice water in a pot. Add the sugar and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring until the sugar and salt dissolve completely. Let the brine cool to room temperature.
  3. Pack the cabbage, carrots, and sliced onion into a clean glass jar or large container, alternating layers. Press down gently to fit everything snugly.
  4. Pour the cooled brine over the vegetables until they are fully submerged.
  5. Place a small plate or weight on top to keep everything under the brine. Cover loosely and leave at room temperature for 2–3 days.
  6. The dưa bắp cải is ready when it tastes pleasantly sour and the cabbage has turned slightly translucent. Transfer to the refrigerator. Serve as a side dish with rice.
