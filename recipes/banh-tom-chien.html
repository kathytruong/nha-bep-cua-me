Bánh Tôm Chiên

Fried Shrimp Cakes

From Mẹ — Oanh

Category
Món Mặn

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

Bột · Batter

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder (bột nghệ) — for golden color
  • 2 cups sparkling water or beer (nước lọc có ga hoặc bia)

Tôm · Shrimp

  • 1 lb medium shrimp, peeled and deveined (leave tails on for presentation)
  • Oil, for deep frying

Để ăn kèm · To Serve

  • Fresh lettuce leaves
  • Fresh herbs — mint, perilla (tía tô), cilantro
  • Pickled carrots and daikon (đồ chua)
  • Nước chấm dipping sauce

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Make the batter: whisk together flour, beaten eggs, salt, and turmeric until smooth. Slowly pour in the sparkling water or beer, stirring gently.
  2. Let the batter rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pot or wok to 350°F. You need enough oil to submerge the shrimp.
  4. Dip each shrimp into the batter, letting the excess drip off. Carefully lower into the hot oil. Fry a few at a time.
  5. Fry for 3–4 minutes, turning once, until golden and crispy. Drain on a wire rack.
  6. Serve immediately with lettuce, fresh herbs, pickled vegetables, and nước chấm.
