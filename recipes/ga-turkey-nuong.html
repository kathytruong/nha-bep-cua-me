Gà Turkey Nướng

Roast Turkey

From Mẹ — Oanh

Prep Time
45 min
Cook Time
3–4 hours
Servings
10–12
Category
Nướng

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 1 whole turkey (13–15 lbs), thawed and patted dry
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 stalks lemongrass (sả), finely minced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (nước mắm)

Nhân gà · Stuffing

  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 3 stalks celery, cut into chunks
  • 2 carrots, cut into chunks
  • 2 ears corn, halved
  • Fresh herbs — thyme, rosemary, sage

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. The night before or early morning, rinse the turkey inside and out. Pat very dry with paper towels — Mẹ always said dry skin is the secret to crispy golden skin.
  2. Make the seasoning paste: mix minced lemongrass, garlic, salt, sugar, fish sauce, black pepper, and oil into a fragrant paste. Rub it all over the turkey, getting under the skin where you can. Let it marinate at least 1 hour, or overnight in the fridge.
  3. Stuff the cavity with quartered onion, celery, carrots, corn, and fresh herbs. These perfume the meat from the inside as it roasts. Tie the legs together with kitchen twine.
  4. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Place turkey breast-side up in a roasting pan. Roast at 400°F for 30 minutes to get the initial browning.
  5. Reduce heat to 350°F. Continue roasting for 2–3 hours, basting every 30 minutes. Mix honey with a little warm water and cornstarch for the basting glaze — this gives Mẹ's turkey its beautiful lacquered look.
  6. When the internal temperature reaches 160°F at the thickest part of the thigh, turn the oven to broil for 3–5 minutes to get the skin deeply golden and crispy. Watch it carefully.
  7. Let the turkey rest for 20–30 minutes before carving. The juices need time to settle back into the meat. Serve with rice or mashed potatoes — Mẹ always made both so everyone was happy.
