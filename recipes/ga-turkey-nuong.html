Gà Turkey Nướng

Roast Turkey

From Mẹ — Oanh

Category
Món Mặn

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

Nhân gà · Stuffing

  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 3 stalks celery, cut into chunks
  • 1 piece ginger (gừng)
  • 3 carrots, cut into chunks
  • 2 ears corn, halved
  • Fresh herbs — thyme, rosemary, sage

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. The night before or early morning, rinse the turkey inside and out. Pat very dry with paper towels.
  2. Make the seasoning paste: mix minced lemongrass, garlic, salt, sugar, fish sauce, black pepper, and oil into a fragrant paste. Rub it all over the turkey, getting under the skin where you can. Let it marinate at least 1 hour, or overnight in the fridge.
  3. Stuff the cavity with quartered onion, celery, carrots, corn, and fresh herbs. These perfume the meat from the inside as it roasts. Tie the legs together with kitchen twine.
  4. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Place turkey breast-side up in a roasting pan. Roast at 400°F for 5–10 minutes to get the initial browning.
  5. Reduce heat to 350°F. Continue roasting for about 3 hours total, basting every 30 minutes. Mix honey with a little warm water and cornstarch for the basting glaze.
  6. When the internal temperature reaches 160°F at the thickest part of the thigh, turn the oven to broil for 3–5 minutes to get the skin deeply golden and crispy. Watch it carefully.
  7. Let the turkey rest for 20–30 minutes before carving. Serve with rice or mashed potatoes.
