Thịt Kho Trứng

Braised Pork & Eggs

From Mẹ Thịnh

Prep Time
30 min
Cook Time
1.5 hours
Servings
4–6
Category
Món Mặn

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • Eggs — boil and peel
  • Pork shoulder — trim excess fat
  • Coconut soda or coconut water
  • Water, to submerge
  • Sugar — a little, if using coconut water instead of coconut soda
  • Fish sauce
  • 2–3 cloves garlic
  • 2–3 shallots

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Boil and peel the eggs. Set aside.
  2. Cut off excess fat from the pork shoulder.
  3. Boil the pork shoulder ahead of time. Pour out the water and rinse clean.
  4. Add coconut soda or coconut water, then fill with water to submerge the pork.
  5. If using coconut water, add a little sugar.
  6. Season with fish sauce, garlic, and shallots.
  7. Braise until the pork is tender and the sauce is rich. Add the eggs toward the end.
