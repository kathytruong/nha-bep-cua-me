Thịt Nướng Marinade

Pork Marinade for Bún Thịt Nướng & Cơm Tấm

From Mẹ Thịnh

Prep Time
20 min + marinate
Cook Time
15 min
Servings
4–6
Category
Món Mặn

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork shoulder, or thin-cut bone-in pork chop (will be drier)
  • 1–2 spoons condensed milk or half-and-half, to tenderize
  • 1 spoon oyster sauce
  • 1½ spoons sugar
  • 1 minced shallot
  • Garlic
  • Black pepper
  • ½ spoon fish sauce
  • Coconut milk — enough to fill to the top of the meat
  • Skewers, for bún thịt nướng

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Cut pork shoulder into fillets. For bún thịt nướng, pound the meat until thin.
  2. Mix the marinade ingredients and coat the meat.
  3. Pour coconut milk over the meat until it just covers the top.
  4. Marinate day-of for as long as possible — at least 4 hours.
  5. For cơm tấm: air fry at 400°F, 7 minutes on each side.
  6. For bún thịt nướng: thread pounded meat onto skewers and broil until charred at the edges.
