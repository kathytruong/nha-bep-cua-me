Bánh Bò Nướng

Baked Honeycomb Cake

From Mẹ — Oanh

Prep Time
15 min
Cook Time
45–50 min
Servings
8–10
Category
Bánh

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

Mẹ kept two versions of this recipe — one from chị Tôi and one from chị Thiết. Both produce that signature honeycomb pattern inside, but each has its own character.

Bản của chị Tôi · Chị Tôi's Version

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 packets vanilla extract
  • Alka-Seltzer (pink/original) — Mẹ's secret for the honeycomb
  • 3 cups tapioca starch (bột năng)

Bản của chị Thiết · Chị Thiết's Version

  • 5 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Alka-Seltzer
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 cups tapioca starch (bột năng)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Cách làm · Instructions

Below are the instructions following chị Tôi's version, which Mẹ used most often.

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch round cake pan or bundt pan with oil or butter.
  2. Beat the eggs and sugar together with an electric mixer on high speed for a full 5 minutes — Mẹ was specific about this timing. The mixture should become very thick, pale, and tripled in volume.
  3. Add the coconut milk and vanilla, mixing on low speed until just combined.
  4. Sift the tapioca starch into the batter and fold in gently until smooth. The batter should be pourable but not too thin.
  5. Crush the Alka-Seltzer tablets into a fine powder — this is the "bí mật" (secret) for creating the honeycomb tunnels inside the cake. Fold it into the batter quickly and pour immediately into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake at 350°F for 45–50 minutes, until the top is deep golden brown and a skewer comes out clean. The top should be slightly caramelized.
  7. Let cool in the pan for 15 minutes, then turn out onto a rack. When sliced, you should see the beautiful honeycomb pattern of air tunnels running through the cake.
  8. Slice into wedges and serve at room temperature. The texture should be chewy and bouncy — that's how you know it's right.

Chị Thiết's variation: Uses more eggs (5 instead of 2) and adds both Alka-Seltzer and baking powder for extra lift. Also adds a tablespoon of oil for moisture. Follow the same method but reduce the sugar beating time as the extra eggs provide more structure. Bake at 350°F for the same time.
