Cơm Chiên

Vietnamese Fried Rice

From Mẹ — Oanh

Category
Cơm & Xôi

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked jasmine rice, day-old and cold (cơm nguội)
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • ½ cup corn kernels
  • ½ cup green peas
  • 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce (xì dầu đen)
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (dầu hào)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon MSG (bột ngọt)
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 stalks green onion, chopped (for garnish)

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Stir-fry the shrimp separately in a little oil until cooked through. Set aside.
  2. Beat the eggs with a little salt until even.
  3. Pour the eggs into a hot oiled pan and scramble quickly.
  4. Add the cold rice into the pan on high heat. Break up any clumps and stir well.
  5. Season the rice with salt, MSG, sugar, dark soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Toss everything together until evenly mixed.
  6. Add the stir-fried shrimp, corn, and peas. Stir to combine well.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onion. Serve hot.
