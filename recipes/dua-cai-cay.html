Dưa Cải Cay

Pickled Mustard Greens

From Mẹ — Oanh

Prep Time
15 min
Cook Time
2–3 days (ferment)
Servings
1 large jar
Category
Đồ Chua

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 1 large bunch mustard greens (cải cay), about 1.5 lbs
  • 2 stalks green onion (hành lá), cut into sections
  • 3–4 shallots (hành tím), thinly sliced
  • 2–3 red horn chilies (ớt sừng đỏ), sliced
  • 2 tablespoons salt (muối)
  • Water, enough to submerge

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Pick off any damaged leaves from the mustard greens. Wash thoroughly. Cut into pieces about 5cm long.
  2. Cut the green onion into sections. Slice the shallots. Cut the red chilies into rounds (khoanh).
  3. Place all ingredients -- mustard greens, green onion, shallots, and chili -- into a clean jar.
  4. Pour salted water (nước muối dưa) into the jar until it covers the surface of the vegetables.
  5. Cover and leave at room temperature for 2–3 days until sour. Transfer to the refrigerator.
