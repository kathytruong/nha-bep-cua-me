Đồ Chua

Pickled Carrot & Daikon

From Mẹ Thịnh

Prep Time
20 min
Cook Time
2 hours rest
Servings
1 jar
Category
Đồ Chua

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 1 medium/small daikon
  • 2 medium carrots
  • 4 tablespoons white sugar
  • 5 tablespoons white vinegar
  • Salt — 1 tbsp for the carrots

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Shred the carrots and toss with 1 tbsp salt in a bowl.
  2. Shred the daikon and add to the carrots.
  3. Rinse the carrots and daikon in boiling hot water.
  4. Transfer to a bowl with the sugar and vinegar. Mix well.
  5. Let sit for a couple of hours, revisiting and stirring occasionally.
  6. Drain excess vinegar and sugar mixture before storing in a glass jar.
