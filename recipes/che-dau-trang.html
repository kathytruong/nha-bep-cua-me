Chè Đậu Trắng

White Bean Sweet Soup

From Mẹ — Oanh

Category
Desserts

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 3 cans white beans (đậu trắng), drained and rinsed
  • 1¼ cups sticky rice (gạo nếp), rinsed
  • 2 cups sugar, or to taste
  • 5⅓ cups water
  • Pinch of salt
  • Coconut cream for drizzling (optional)

Phần lớn hơn · Larger Batch

  • 3 cans white beans
  • 2 cups sticky rice
  • 4 cups sugar
  • 7 cups water

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Rinse the sticky rice several times until the water runs clear.
  2. In a large pot, bring the water to a boil. Add the sticky rice and cook until the rice is completely soft.
  3. Drain and rinse the canned white beans. You can microwave the beans briefly to soften them. Add them to the pot with the cooked sticky rice. Stir gently to combine.
  4. When the mixture comes to a boil, add sugar and a pinch of salt. Stir until the sugar is fully dissolved.
  5. Simmer everything together for another 5–10 minutes so the flavors meld. The chè should be thick but still pourable.
  6. Serve warm in small bowls, drizzled with coconut cream if you like.
