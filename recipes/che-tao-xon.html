Chè Tào Xọn

Mung Bean Dessert

From Mẹ — Oanh

Prep Time
20 min + soak
Cook Time
30 min
Servings
6–8
Category
Chè

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 200g mung beans (đậu xanh), husked and split
  • 100g tapioca starch (bột năng)
  • 300g sugar (đường cát)
  • Coconut cream (nước cốt dừa)
  • 1.5 liters water

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Steam the mung beans until fully cooked and tender.
  2. Add the sugar to 1.5 liters of water in a large pot. Bring to a boil, stirring until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Add the tapioca starch gradually, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and becomes glossy and slightly translucent.
  4. Fold in the steamed mung beans. Stir to distribute them evenly.
  5. Serve topped with coconut cream.
