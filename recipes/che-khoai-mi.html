Chè Khoai Mì Nướng

Baked Cassava Cake

From Mẹ — Oanh

Prep Time
30 min + soak
Cook Time
45–60 min
Servings
8–10
Category
Chè

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 2 pounds cassava (khoai mì), peeled
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk (nước cốt dừa)
  • Sugar, to taste (đường)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Aluminum foil for covering

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Peel the cassava and soak it overnight in cold water. This softens the flesh and removes any bitterness.
  2. Drain the cassava and squeeze out as much water as possible. You can grate it finely or pulse it in a food processor until it reaches a coarse, crumbly texture. Squeeze again through cheesecloth if it still feels very wet.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the grated cassava, sugar, coconut milk, and salt. Mix and knead everything together until well combined.
  4. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking pan or line it with parchment paper.
  5. Pour the cassava mixture into the prepared pan and press it down evenly. Cover tightly with aluminum foil — this traps the steam and helps the inside cook through before the top browns.
  6. Bake covered with foil. When the cake is set inside, remove the foil and continue baking until the top turns golden brown.
  7. Let it cool in the pan before cutting. Cut into squares or diamond shapes and serve.
