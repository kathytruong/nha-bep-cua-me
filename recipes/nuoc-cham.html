Nước Chấm

Vietnamese Dipping Sauce

From Mẹ — Oanh

Prep Time
5 min
Cook Time
None
Servings
1 bowl
Category
Nước Chấm

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons sugar (đường)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (xì dầu)
  • 1 tablespoon soy paste (nước tương)
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce (tương ớt)
  • 5 tablespoons fish sauce (nước mắm)

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Combine the sugar, soy sauce, soy paste, chili sauce, and fish sauce in a small bowl. Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  2. Taste and adjust to your liking.
  3. Serve alongside bột chiên, gỏi cuốn, bánh cuốn, or other dishes.
