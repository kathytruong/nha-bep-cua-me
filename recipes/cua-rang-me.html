Cua Rang Me

Tamarind Crab

From Mẹ — Oanh

Category
Món Mặn

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 2 whole live crabs (about 2 lbs each), cleaned
  • ½ cup tamarind paste (me chua), dissolved in 1 cup warm water and strained
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 shallots (hành tím), minced
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (nước mắm)
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • ½ teaspoon MSG (bột ngọt)
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Cut the crab into pieces. Crack the claws. Scoop out the roe (gạch cua) into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok with garlic and shallots.
  3. Add the crab and stir-fry on high heat.
  4. Add fish sauce, sugar, and MSG.
  5. Stir the tamarind water until boiling, then pour about half a bowl over the crab. Reduce heat to low. If too sour, adjust.
  6. Cover and cook for a few minutes. Add the reserved crab roe on top.
  7. Cover again briefly, then serve.
