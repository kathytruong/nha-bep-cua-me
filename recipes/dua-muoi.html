Dưa Muối

Vietnamese Pickled Vegetables

From Mẹ — Oanh

Prep Time
15 min
Cook Time
3–4 days (ferment)
Servings
1 large jar
Category
Đồ Chua

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 7 cucumbers (dưa leo)
  • 4 tablespoons salt (muối)
  • Enough water to submerge the vegetables
  • Sugar, to taste (for the second stage)

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Wash the cucumbers. Cut in half and slice into pieces. Place in a clean glass jar.
  2. Dissolve the salt in water to make a brine. Pour the salted water over the cucumbers until fully submerged.
  3. Press the cucumbers down with a plate or heavy bowl to keep them under the brine. Cover loosely.
  4. Let the jar sit at room temperature for 3 days.
  5. After 3 days, taste the dưa. If it's sour enough, add a little sugar to balance the flavor. Add more sugar to taste as needed.
  6. Once the pickles reach your desired sourness (usually 3–4 days total), move the jar to the refrigerator. Serve alongside rice, soup, or braised dishes.
