Chè Khoai Môn

Taro Sweet Soup

From Mẹ — Oanh

Category
Desserts

Nguyên liệu · Ingredients

  • 1 pound taro (khoai môn), peeled and cut into small pieces
  • 1 cup sticky rice (gạo nếp), rinsed
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut cream (nước cốt dừa)
  • 2 cups sugar, or to taste (đường)
  • 6–10 cups water
  • Pinch of salt (muối)

Cách làm · Instructions

  1. Rinse the sticky rice and soak it for at least 1 hour. This helps it cook evenly and become properly soft in the chè.
  2. Peel the taro, cut into small pieces, and steam until tender.
  3. Rinse the sticky rice. Bring the water to a boil. Add the sticky rice and cook until soft, stirring gently.
  4. Add the sugar and salt. Stir until the sugar dissolves. Reduce heat to low.
  5. When the taro is nearly done, add it to the pot. Stir gently.
  6. Add the coconut cream and stir to combine. Simmer together briefly.
  7. Serve warm in bowls.
